Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if it rains?

+

We monitor weather conditions closely. If rain is forecasted, we'll work with you to reschedule your booking at no extra cost, or provide a full refund if preferred.

How much space is needed?

+

Our castles typically need a flat area of 15x15 feet minimum. We'll assess your space during booking to ensure the perfect fit for your chosen castle.

What about safety measures?

+

All our castles are fully insured and safety certified. We provide safety mats, secure anchoring, and clear usage guidelines. Adult supervision is required at all times.

How long is the rental period?

+

Standard rental is 6 hours. This includes setup and takedown time. Extended rental periods are available upon request at additional cost.

Do you provide setup and takedown?

+

Yes! Professional setup and takedown is included in all our rental packages. Our trained staff will handle everything for you.

What is the booking process?

+

Simply choose your castle, select a date, and make a 25% deposit to secure your booking. We'll contact you to confirm details and arrange inspection if needed.
